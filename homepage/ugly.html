About

History

I am the executive chairman and CEO of Microsoft, succeeding Steve Ballmer in 2014 as CEO and John W. Thompson in 2021 as chairman. Before becoming CEO, I was the executive vice president of Microsoft's cloud and enterprise group, responsible for building and running the company's computing platforms.
